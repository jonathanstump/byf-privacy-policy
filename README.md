# Privacy Policy for Bet Your Friends: Social Bets

*Last updated: April 28, 2026*

Bet Your Friends: Social Bets ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Bet Your Friends mobile application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your username and email address. We collect records of in-app purchases and virtual currency (GLP coins) transactions. We collect data about bets you create and participate in, including bet outcomes and group membership. We collect standard device and usage data to operate the app.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and maintain the app, process in-app purchases, manage your account and virtual currency balance, enable social features like group bets, and improve the app experience.

## In-App Purchases

We process in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. We verify purchase receipts server-side to credit your account. We do not store your payment information — all payment processing is handled by Apple.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to operate the service (such as our hosting provider) or as required by law.

## Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
